Much more is this so in life. A slave thou art: free speech is not for thee. And my heart laughed within. And virtue they will curse, speaking harsh words. To look for the fig in winter is a madman's act: such is he who looks for his child when it is no longer allowed. When a man kisses his child, said Epictetus, he should whisper to himself, to-morrow perchance thou wilt die.
