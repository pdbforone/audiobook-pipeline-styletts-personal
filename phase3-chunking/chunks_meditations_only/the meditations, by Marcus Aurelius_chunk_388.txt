And it is in thy power also; or say, who hinders thee? Labour not as one who is wretched, nor yet as one who would be pitied or admired: but direct thy will to one thing only, to put thyself in motion and to check thyself, as the social reason requires. To-day I have got out of all trouble, or rather I have cast out all trouble, for it was not outside, but within and in my opinions.
